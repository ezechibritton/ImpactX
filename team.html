Impact X Team

Comprised of the Founding Members, and the Investment Team, Impact X comprises of seasoned professionals from an ethically diverse backgrounds. Many have worked with some of the largest brands in the world. They are serial entrepreneurs, institutional investors, investment bankers and university professors from the world’s most prestigious educational institutions.

Our team of accomplished business leaders have:

  • Successfully managed more than £12 billion
  • Built large-scale companies
  • Created meaningful exits across diverse industries
  • Worked closely with underrepresented entrepreneurs
  • Generated solid returns for investors

Impact X Investment Team

Eric Collins

CEO/General Partner

Eric is a serial entrepreneur and has served on boards and in the C-Suite of fast growth technology companies since 2001. Eric's experiences include AI, SaaS based, mobile and health technology companies. He has helped sell organisations to listed companies including Microsoft and Nuance. He has been named one of the most powerful Black and BAME business people in the UK by the Power-list and the Financial times.

Ric Lewis

Chair

Ric is Tristan Capital Partners’ Founder, Chairman, Co-Chief Executive and Chair of the Tristan Investment Committee. Tristan is a London based real estate investment firm with approximately £11 billion under management, making it the largest private Black owned business in the UK.

Ric attended Dartmouth University and Harvard Business School.

Ursula Burns

Vice-Chair, Digital and Technology

Ursula was the CEO of Xerox, the first black woman CEO to head a Fortune 500 company. She is currently the executive chair of VEON, a listed Europe-based integrated telco operating throughout Europe, Asia and the Africa. Burns’ board service includes Uber, American Express, ExxonMobil, Ford Foundation, Nestle and the New York City Ballet. Ursula was appointed by President Barack Obama to head the STEM program at the White House.

Lenny Henry

Vice-Chair, Creative

Sir Lenworth (Lenny) George Henry CBE is an acclaimed English creative who co-founded the charity Comic Relief which since its inception has raised over £1 billion. Lenny is known for his acting on stage, in film and especially for his comedy and television work. His TV credits include the hit comedy Chef! as well as The Magicians for BBC One. Lenny was knighted in 2015 and given the Alan Clarke at the 2016 BAFTAs.

Joe Voyticky

Vice-Chair, Health and Lifestyle

Joe is CEO of Max International, LLC leading the expansion of the health and lifestyle network marketing business to Europe, Asia and Africa. Before Max, Joe spent 14 years as a finance lawyer in New York City. Joe has degrees from Union College and Harvard Law School.

Paul Cleal

Vice-Chair, Operations

Paul acts as an adviser, board member or mentor to organisations and individuals who want to make a positive change happen. He brings a wealth of experience in leadership and strategy gained from 30 years working in both private and public sectors.

During 16 years as a partner at Price Waterhouse Coopers, the global professional services firm, he held a range of leadership positions including at main board level in both the UK and Africa practices. He has led and grown successful businesses within the Government & Public Sector practice and Africa Business Group.
